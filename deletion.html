The app only uses Instagram and Facebook authorization to publish content on behalf of the account owner. No personal information, messages, contacts or media files are permanently stored. ## How to request data deletion If you want to remove all data related to our application, you can: 1. Remove the app from your Facebook account: Settings → Security and Login → Business Integrations → Remove App 2. Or send a request to: **[email@example.com](mailto:email@example.com)** We will confirm deletion within 48 hours. ## What happens after deletion After removing the application access: * all tokens become invalid * the app can no longer access your Instagram account * no data remains associated with your account
